ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Brian! I am a UX/UI designer with a Computer Science degree with experience crafting digital products in healthcare and education.


With experience in both entrepreneurial and corporate environments, I have a proven ability to assess market conditions, support discovery and provide valuable design direction on existing products while supporting development efforts. My professional and educational interests have taken me to many cities from Boston, Detroit, Lansing and Salt Lake City to Houghton, the home of Michigan Technological University (MTU).

As a continuous learner, I am passionate about opportunities to expand my professional growth and development and make a sustainable contribution to the field of UX/UI.
